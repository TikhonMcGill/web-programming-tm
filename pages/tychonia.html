Tychonia

Tychonia is the Capital City of Tikhon the Great's Empire, and the Background Image for this Website

Notable features of Tychonia:

  1. Tychonia has over 3 million people living in it
  2. Tychonia houses "The Citadel", the world's tallest building
  3. Tychonia is home to the Tychonic Culture House, a Bastion of Neo-Classical culture, where Plays and Operas are composed in Greek and Latin
  4. Despite its urban nature, Tychonia is fully self-sustaining with dozens of underground Hydroponic Farms providing food to its population
  5. It cost $36 Billion to construct Tychonia's initial settlement
  6. The first Tychonic child was born in Tychonia on April 4th, 2034
